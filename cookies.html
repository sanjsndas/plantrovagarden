Cookies Policy

Last Updated: January 15, 2023

This Cookies Policy explains what cookies are and how we use them on our website. You should read this policy to understand what types of cookies we use, the information we collect using cookies, and how that information is used.

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to the site owners. Cookies cannot be used to run programs or deliver viruses to your computer.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies primarily to enhance your experience on our website. They help us recognize your device and remember your preferences when you return to our site. Some cookies are essential for the functioning of the website, while others help us analyze visitor behavior and improve our services.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Our website uses several types of cookies including strictly necessary cookies, performance cookies, functionality cookies, and targeting cookies. Each type serves a different purpose and collects different types of information about your browsing behavior.

4. Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for you to browse our website and use its features. Without these cookies, services like shopping baskets and secure logins would not work properly. They ensure the website functions as designed and must be enabled for you to use our services effectively.

5. Performance Cookies

These cookies collect information about how visitors use our website, including which pages are visited most often and any error messages visitors receive. They help us improve how the website works by measuring how visitors interact with our content.

6. Functionality Cookies

These cookies allow our website to remember choices you make (such as your username, language preference, or region) and provide enhanced, more personal features. These can also be used to remember changes you've made to text size, fonts, and other customizable web page elements.

7. Targeting Cookies

These cookies track your visits to our website, the pages you visit, and the links you follow. We may share this information with third parties (such as advertisers) to help deliver more relevant advertising to you when you visit other websites.

8. First-Party Cookies

These are cookies placed directly by our website that we are visiting. Only we have access to the information collected by these cookies which are typically used for functionality purposes like remembering your login details or preferences.

9. Third-Party Cookies

These cookies are placed by domains other than our own when we include elements from other sites on our pages (like advertisements). They are often used for tracking and online advertising purposes.

10. Session Cookies

These cookies are temporary and only remain in your browser until you leave our website. They are typically used to maintain your session as you navigate through different pages on our site.

11. Persistent Cookies

These cookies remain on your device for the period specified in the cookie or until you manually delete them. They are used to remember your preferences and actions beyond the current session.

12. Analytics Cookies

We use analytics cookies to understand how visitors interact with our website. These cookies help provide information about metrics such as number of visitors, bounce rate, traffic sources, and other key performance indicators.

13. Advertising Cookies

These cookies enable us to serve you with advertisements that may be relevant to your interests based on your browsing history. They are used to display targeted advertising to you across different websites.

14. Cookie Duration

Different cookies have different lifespans. Some cookies expire when you close your browser (session cookies), while others may remain on your device for months or even years (persistent cookies), unless you delete them manually.

15. Consent for Cookies

When you first visit our website, we ask for your consent to use cookies through our cookie banner. You can choose to accept all cookies, reject all cookies, or customize your cookie preferences.

16. Managing Cookies

You can control and/or delete cookies as you wish through your browser settings. You can delete all cookies that are already on your device and set most browsers to prevent them from being placed.

17. Browser Controls

Most web browsers allow some control of cookies through browser settings. You can adjust your browser settings to refuse cookies or alert you when cookies are being sent. However, if you disable cookies, some features of our website may not function properly.

18. Do Not Track Signals

Some browsers offer a "Do Not Track" feature that signals to websites you visit that you do not want to have your online activity tracked. Our website does not currently respond to Do Not Track signals.

19. GDPR Compliance

For visitors from the European Union, our use of cookies complies with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). We ensure that we have a legal basis for processing personal data collected through cookies when required.

20. CCPA Compliance

For visitors from California, our cookie practices comply with the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). California residents have the right to opt-out of the sale of their personal information, including certain uses of cookies.

21. Cookie Declaration

We maintain a detailed cookie declaration that lists all the cookies used on our website, their purposes, durations, and whether they are first-party or third-party cookies. This declaration is available through our cookie preference center.

22. Changes to Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

23. Essential Website Functions

Certain cookies are necessary for core website functions such as maintaining your logged-in status, keeping items in your shopping cart, and ensuring security. These cookies don't require your consent but are automatically stored when you visit our site.

24. Personalized Content

Cookies help us deliver personalized content based on your browsing behavior and preferences. This includes showing you relevant products or services and remembering your language or region settings.

25. Social Media Cookies

We use social media plugins that may set cookies when you interact with our content through platforms like Facebook, Twitter, or Instagram. These cookies allow social media platforms to track your browsing habits across websites.

26. Performance Optimization

Performance cookies help us understand how visitors interact with different pages on our site so we can optimize the user experience. They provide insights into which pages load slowly or receive errors.

27. Preference Management

You can manage your cookie preferences through our cookie settings panel. This allows you to enable or disable different categories of cookies according to your preferences while still accessing most website functionality.

28. Flash Cookies

Our website does not currently use Flash cookies (also called Local Shared Objects). These are different from browser cookies and require different management techniques in your browser settings or Flash player settings.

29. Mobile Cookies

When accessing our website from mobile devices, similar tracking technologies may be used to collect information about your mobile browsing behavior. Mobile cookies function similarly to standard cookies but are optimized for mobile devices.

30. Cookie Consent Management

You can manage your cookie consent preferences at any time by revisiting our cookie consent banner. This allows you to change your choices regarding the use of cookies on our site.

31. Cookie Policy Contact Information

If you have any questions about our cookie policy or practices, please contact us at support@plantrova.com. We are here to assist you with any inquiries you may have regarding our use of cookies.

32. Cookie Policy Updates

We encourage you to review our cookie policy periodically for any updates. Changes will be reflected on this page, and your continued use of our website signifies your acceptance of the updated policy.

33. Cookie Preferences for New Users

New users visiting our website will see the cookie consent banner upon their first visit. This ensures that they are informed about our cookie practices and can make an informed choice.

34. Cookie Policy for Children

Our website is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children, and if we become aware of such data, we will take steps to delete it.

35. Cookie Policy for Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services that set cookies on our behalf. These services help us analyze website traffic, deliver advertisements, and provide other functionalities. We do not control these cookies and recommend reviewing their privacy policies.

36. Cookie Policy for Analytics Tools

We utilize analytics tools that may set cookies to collect data about user interactions on our site. This information helps us improve our website and user experience by understanding how visitors engage with our content.

37. Cookie Policy for Advertising Partners

Our advertising partners may use cookies to deliver targeted ads based on your interests. These cookies track your browsing behavior across different websites to provide more relevant advertising.

38. Cookie Policy for Affiliate Programs

If you access our website through an affiliate link, cookies may be set to track referrals and commissions. These cookies help us manage our affiliate relationships and ensure proper compensation.

39. Cookie Policy for User Experience

Cookies enhance user experience by remembering your preferences and settings. This allows for a more personalized browsing experience, making it easier for you to navigate our website.

40. Final Thoughts on Cookies

Understanding our cookie policy is essential for a seamless experience on our website. We appreciate your attention to this matter and encourage you to reach out with any questions or concerns regarding our use of cookies.

We use cookies to enhance your experience on our website. By clicking "Accept", you consent to our use of cookies. You can manage your preferences at any time.
